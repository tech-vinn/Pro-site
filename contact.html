What documents do I need to rent?+
Typically you'll need a valid passport or national ID, proof of income or employment letter, and references from a previous landlord if available. Some landlords may request bank statements for premium properties.
How much is the security deposit?+
Security deposits are usually equivalent to 1–2 months' rent, depending on the landlord and property type. All deposits are held in a secure client account and returned within 30 days of vacating, subject to property condition checks.
Does Aura Homes charge a fee to tenants?+
No. Our agency fees are always paid by the landlord, not the tenant. You will never be asked for a fee simply to view, apply for, or rent a property through Aura Homes.
Can I do a virtual tour?+
Yes. All our listed properties include a virtual tour option. You can either view a pre-recorded walkthrough or book a live video tour with one of our agents at a time that suits you.
How quickly can I move in?+
Once your application is approved and the lease is signed, most properties are available within 48–72 hours. Some furnished properties can be ready same-day.
I'm a landlord — how do I list my property?+
Contact us via the form above or call us directly. We'll send a property consultant to conduct a complimentary inspection and valuation. Listings typically go live within 5 business days of signing our management agreement.
Are pets allowed in listed properties?+
This varies by landlord. We clearly label all pet-friendly properties with a "Pet Friendly" badge. If a property you're interested in isn't listed as pet friendly, contact us — we can sometimes negotiate with the landlord.
