Our Story

Who We Are,
Why We Care

Aura Homes was founded on a simple belief: finding the right home should feel as elevated as the home itself.

Our Mission

Elevating the
Rental Experience

"We believe every person deserves a home that matches their ambition."

Aura Homes was born out of frustration with an industry that treated renting as transactional. We reimagined every step — from the first search to the day you get your keys — with transparency, care, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today we manage over 320 premium properties across Dar es Salaam, serving expats, professionals, and families who expect more from where they live.
