About This Property

The Oasis Penthouse sits atop one of Masaki's most prestigious residential towers, offering sweeping panoramic views of the Indian Ocean and the city skyline. With floor-to-ceiling windows flooding every room with natural light, the apartment has been meticulously designed to blend understated luxury with everyday comfort.

The open-plan living and dining area flows seamlessly onto a private terrace — perfect for entertaining or quiet mornings with a view. The chef's kitchen is fitted with premium European appliances, stone countertops, and custom cabinetry. Each of the three bedrooms features an en-suite bathroom with heated floors and rainfall showers.
